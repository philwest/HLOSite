Resources

What resources are available to me?

Here are some online sources to help you as you're looking toward filing your bankruptcy.

Obtaining Your Credit Report

The Annual Credit Report website is the best place to obtain your free, annual credit report from the three major credit bureaus.


Valuing Your Car

The NADA Guides website will help you determine the value of your new or used car.


Valuing Your Property

The Travis County Appraisal District website will help you determine the value of your property in Travis County.
The Hays Central Appraisal District website will help you determine the value of your property in Hays County.
The Williamson Central Appraisal District website will help you determine the value of your property in Williamson County.
The Burnet County Appraisal District website will help you determine the value of your property in Burnet County.
The Bastrop Central Appraisal District website will help you determine the value of your property in Bastrop County.


Loan Modification Resources

The Home Affordable Modification Program (HAMP) website details a federal program that can help homeowners lower their monthly mortgage payments.
Bank of America customers can seek home loan modification at this website.
Wells Fargo customers can seek home loan modification at this website.
Ocwen Loan Servicing customers can seek home loan modification at this website.


Internal Revenue Service Assistance

For prior tax returns (also referred to as transcripts), visit this page of the IRS website to look them up and order them. (Allow 5-10 business days for copies to be sent by mail; online versions can be downloaded immediately.)


Track Your Chapter 13 Bankruptcy

You can access your Chapter 13 bankruptcy case through the National Data Center website. (Debtor registration and authentication required for site access.)
