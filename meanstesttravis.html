Means Test

To determine whether someone qualifies for Chapter 7 bankruptcy, a means test is required, which looks at a person's income, debts, and expenses to determine whether he or she has the means to pay off those debts.


While we recommend coming into our office, so we can take you through all the steps in the means test, knowing how your income compares to the median income is an important first step in the process. Use this tool to compare your income to the median income in your county.
